💡 INJURY PREVENTION TIPS

🔥
Warm up every session. 5–10 minutes of dynamic stretching prevents 60% of common gym injuries. Never skip it.
😴
Sleep is recovery. Muscles grow during sleep, not during workouts. 7–9 hours is non-negotiable for progress.
📈
Progressive overload safely. Increase weight by max 5–10% per week. Jumping too fast causes tears and strains.
💧
Stay hydrated. Dehydration reduces muscle function by 20–30% and significantly increases cramp risk.
🗓️
Plan rest days. At least 1–2 rest days per week. Overtraining increases cortisol and breaks down muscle tissue.
